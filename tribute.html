Tribute to Maharana Pratap

About Maharana Pratap

Pratap Singh I, popularly known as Maharana Pratap (c. 9 May 1540 – 19 January 1597), was a Hindu Rajput king of Mewar from the Sisodia dynasty

Some Interesting Facts About Maharana Pratap

  • Maharana Pratap was the 13th King of the Mewar region in present day Rajasthan.
  • He was born on 9th May 1540 to Udai Singh II and Jaiwanta Bai.
  • When many Rajput Kings had convened with the Mughals, Maharana rejected Akbar’s proposal of allegiance.
  • Battle of Haldighati was fought between Maharana Pratap and Man Singh I of Amer, representing Akbar’s forces.
  • The battle was fierce and Mewar forces were injured in large numbers.
  • Despite the casualties, the Mughals failed to capture Maharana Pratap.
  • Maharana Pratap died aged 56 on 19th January 1597, due to injuries sustained in a hunting expedition.
